Privacy Policy

CanBeWell is a mobile health application developed by Emory University to support research studies focused on promoting healthy lifestyle behaviors and improving health outcomes among individuals affected by cancer.

Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected through CanBeWell, how it is used, how it is protected, and your rights as a participant.

Information We Collect

When you use CanBeWell, we may collect the following types of information as part of approved research studies:

  • Account information: Name, email address, or study ID.
  • Self-reported data: Survey responses related to symptoms, quality of life, diet, physical activity, or other health behaviors.
  • Lifestyle and activity data: Step counts or sedentary time collected through wearable devices (e.g., Fitbit), if applicable.
  • Dietary information: Food photos and related dietary data entered through the app.
  • App usage data: Login activity and module completion.

Note: CanBeWell does not collect financial information, contact lists, call history, or unrelated personal data.

How Your Information Is Used

Information collected through CanBeWell is used solely for research and study-related purposes, including:

  • Conducting approved clinical or behavioral research studies.
  • Delivering personalized, study-related content and feedback.
  • Monitoring study engagement and adherence.
  • Analyzing aggregated data to improve research interventions and scientific understanding.
  • Enhancing the functionality and usability of the app.

Your information will not be used for advertising or commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Data collected through CanBeWell are stored on secure, password-protected servers maintained by Emory University or approved service providers.
  • Data transmission is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols (e.g., HTTPS/TLS).
